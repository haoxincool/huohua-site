火花 · 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 23 日

English version below ↓

火花没有服务器，也没有账号体系。
你的日记只存在于你的手机上，我们无法看到、也从不收集任何数据。

1. 我们收集哪些数据？

不收集任何数据。火花不含用户注册、登录或账号功能，不集成任何统计、广告或行为分析 SDK，不向任何服务器发送你的个人信息。开发者不知道你是谁，也无从知道你写了什么。

2. 你的日记内容保存在哪里？

  • 你的文字、语音、照片、视频、位置标注等全部记录，仅保存在你自己设备的本地存储中；
  • AI 功能（回应、情绪识别、日/周/月度回顾）由在你设备上运行的本地模型完成，内容不会离开你的手机；
  • 语音转文字使用 Apple 提供的设备端语音识别，同样在本地完成。

3. 系统权限的用途

  • 麦克风与语音识别：录制语音日记并转为文字，处理均在本地；
  • 相机与相册：拍摄或选取照片、视频插入日记；
  • 位置（可选）：为当条记录标注地点；
  • 面容 ID（可选）：在本机验证身份以保护日记，生物特征数据由 iOS 系统管理，App 无法读取。

以上权限均可拒绝或随时在系统设置中关闭，仅影响对应功能。

4. 数据备份与删除

  • App 内置「备份与恢复」功能，可将全部数据导出为文件，去向完全由你决定（AirDrop、U 盘、本机文件等）；
  • AI 模型文件已排除在 iCloud 备份之外，不占用你的 iCloud 空间；
  • 删除 App 即删除设备上的全部数据。请在删除前先行备份。

5. 第三方共享

不适用。火花不与任何第三方共享数据——因为我们从一开始就没有你的数据。

6. 儿童隐私

火花不面向 14 周岁以下儿童设计。由于 App 不收集任何数据，我们也不会收集儿童的个人信息。

7. 政策变更

若未来功能变化涉及数据处理方式调整（例如可选的云同步），我们会在此页面更新说明，并在 App 内显著提示。「日记内容默认仅存本地」的原则不会改变。

8. 联系我们

对本政策有任何疑问，请联系开发者：haoxincool@126.com

Huohua (Spark) · Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

Huohua has no servers and no accounts.
Your journal lives only on your device. We cannot see it, and we never collect anything.

1. What data do we collect?

None. Huohua has no registration, login, or account system. It contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and sends no personal information to any server. The developer does not know who you are, nor what you write.

2. Where is your journal stored?

  • All your entries — text, voice recordings, photos, videos, and location tags — are stored only in your device's local storage;
  • AI features (replies, mood insights, daily/weekly/monthly reviews) are powered by an on-device model running locally on your phone. Your content never leaves your device;
  • Speech-to-text uses Apple's on-device speech recognition, which also runs entirely locally.

3. System permissions

  • Microphone & Speech Recognition: to record voice entries and transcribe them locally;
  • Camera & Photo Library: to capture or attach photos and videos;
  • Location (optional): to tag where an entry was written;
  • Face ID (optional): to protect your journal on-device. Biometric data is managed by iOS and never accessible to the app.

All permissions can be declined or revoked anytime in Settings; only the corresponding feature is affected.

4. Backup & deletion

  • The built-in Backup & Restore feature exports all your data as files — where they go is entirely up to you (AirDrop, USB drive, local files, etc.);
  • AI model files are excluded from iCloud backup and do not consume your iCloud storage;
  • Deleting the app deletes all data on the device. Please back up first.

5. Third-party sharing

Not applicable. Huohua shares data with no one — because we never have your data in the first place.

6. Children's privacy

Huohua is not designed for children under 14. As the app collects no data, no children's personal information is ever collected.

7. Changes to this policy

If future features change how data is handled (e.g., optional cloud sync), we will update this page and clearly notify you in the app. The principle that your journal stays local by default will not change.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact the developer: haoxincool@126.com
